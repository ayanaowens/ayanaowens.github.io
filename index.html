La Moulade

La Moulade is a design company based in London. They specialize in digital design on many platforms. As well, they have won CSS Awards for their website design.
Scrolling through their webpage, elements move in relation to where you are on the page. There is also a sound effect of a bike as you are halfway down the page. At the bottom of the page there are little shoes that follow the path of the cursor on the page.

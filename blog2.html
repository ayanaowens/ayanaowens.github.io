Design for WWW

Fall 2015

A website by Ayana Owens


Sept 15, 2015

Dadaab Stories is a website that recounts the stories of peoples from one of the biggest refugee camps on the world. Each page has a moving gif in the background of the camp and the people in it. The most intersting part of this website is how smoothly it loads all of these large images and smoothly loops them. As well, the page overall was designed very nicely and is easy to navigate.

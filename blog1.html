A website by Ayana Owens


Sept 8, 2015

La Moulade is a design company based in London. They specialize in digital design on many platforms. As well, they have won CSS Awards for their website design.
The reason for choosing this website was because of the design elements that makes it up. Scrolling through their webpage, elements move in relation to where you are on the page. The movement of images and text make this webpage dynamic and interesting to navigate.
